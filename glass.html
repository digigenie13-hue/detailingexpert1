What is Professional Glass Coating?

Advanced Hydrophobic Protection for Automotive Glass

Professional Glass Coating is an advanced hydrophobic treatment that chemically bonds with your vehicle's windshield and exterior glass surfaces. It creates an invisible protective layer that repels water, dirt, road grime, and other contaminants while improving visibility and making routine cleaning much easier.

At Detailing Experts, our Glass Coating service is professionally applied at our Vadodara and Godhra studios using premium-grade products. The coating enhances driving safety during rain, minimizes hard water stains, reduces wiper friction, and delivers crystal-clear visibility for everyday driving.

Our Professional Glass Coating Process

01

Vehicle Inspection

We inspect your windshield and exterior glass to identify water spots, scratches, contamination, and surface defects before beginning the treatment.

02

Glass Cleaning

The glass is thoroughly cleaned using professional-grade products to remove dirt, grease, road film, and other contaminants.

03

Surface Preparation

We prepare the glass surface to ensure the coating bonds properly, creating maximum durability and long-lasting performance.

04

Glass Polishing

Hard water stains, light wiper marks, and minor imperfections are removed using professional glass polishing techniques.

05

Coating Application

Our premium hydrophobic Glass Coating is carefully applied to achieve uniform coverage and exceptional water-repellent performance.

06

Curing

The coating is allowed to cure under controlled conditions, ensuring a strong chemical bond with the glass surface.

07

Final Inspection

Our technicians perform a final quality inspection to verify complete coverage, clarity, and finish before vehicle delivery.

08

Vehicle Delivery

Your vehicle is delivered with aftercare guidance to help maintain the performance and longevity of the Glass Coating.
