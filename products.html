Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our product lines and application guidelines.

The right option depends on your vehicle, usage, current surface condition, and protection goals. Paint protection film focuses on physical surface protection, while ceramic and graphene coatings focus on gloss, hydrophobic performance, and easier maintenance. Our team can recommend a suitable option after inspecting your vehicle.

Service life varies by the selected product or package, surface preparation, vehicle usage, washing methods, storage conditions, and maintenance. Please ask our studio team for the expected service life and care requirements of the specific option recommended for your vehicle.

Choose based on the type of protection you need. PPF is intended to help protect painted surfaces from everyday road exposure and minor abrasion, while coatings focus on gloss, hydrophobic behaviour, and easier maintenance. Some owners may choose both after professional consultation.

Selected car care and maintenance products may be available through our Vadodara and Godhra studios. Please contact the nearest studio to confirm current availability and suitable products for your vehicle.

Warranty availability and terms depend on the specific product, service package, installer requirements, and applicable manufacturer or studio conditions. We will explain any relevant warranty coverage before you confirm the service.

Use safe washing methods, suitable car care products, and the aftercare guidance provided for your specific coating. Avoid harsh cleaning methods that may reduce surface performance, and contact our team if you are unsure which products to use.

Follow the installation-specific aftercare instructions provided by our studio. Safe washing, careful cleaning around film edges, and prompt removal of contaminants can help maintain the film. Contact us before using unfamiliar chemicals or abrasive products.

Paint protection film and professional coating services should be applied by trained technicians with appropriate preparation. Selected maintenance products and accessories may be suitable for owner use when used according to the product instructions and our guidance.

Neither option is automatically best for every vehicle. The suitable choice depends on the specific product, your expectations, budget, vehicle usage, and maintenance routine. Our team can explain the available options without relying on unsupported one-size-fits-all claims.

Some compatible coating products can be applied over certain paint protection films. Compatibility depends on the film and coating system, so the surface should be inspected and the specific products confirmed before application.

Contact our Vadodara or Godhra studio by phone or WhatsApp and tell us which product category you are interested in. Our team can confirm current availability and recommend an appropriate option.

Delivery options may vary by product and location. Please contact our Vadodara or Godhra studio to confirm whether the selected item is available for collection or delivery.

Return or exchange eligibility depends on the product condition, packaging, purchase channel, and applicable store policy. Please confirm the current terms with the studio before purchase, especially for chemical or opened products.

Suitability depends on the specific product and protected surface. Use products recommended for your coating or film and follow the applicable instructions. Our team can help you choose a suitable maintenance option.

Frequency depends on the specific product, vehicle usage, washing routine, and protection system. Follow the product instructions and the aftercare plan provided for your vehicle rather than using a fixed schedule for every product.
