Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products, professional services, and aftercare guidance.

The right option depends on your priorities. Paint protection film is suited to owners seeking physical protection from everyday road exposure, while ceramic and graphene coatings focus on gloss, water repellency, and easier maintenance. Our team can recommend an option after inspecting the vehicle and discussing how it is used.

Choose based on the type of protection you need. PPF is intended for physical surface protection, while ceramic and graphene coatings are chosen mainly for gloss, water repellency, and easier cleaning. Some owners combine services after a professional assessment.

Selected maintenance products and accessories may be available through our Vadodara and Godhra studios. Please contact the team to confirm current availability before visiting or placing an enquiry.

Warranty coverage depends on the selected product or service. Our team will explain the applicable terms before work begins or a product is supplied.

Use careful washing methods, suitable car-care products, and follow the aftercare guidance provided for the specific service. Avoid harsh cleaning methods that may damage the protected surface.

Services such as PPF, ceramic coating, and graphene coating require professional preparation and application. Selected maintenance products and accessories can be used by vehicle owners when suitable instructions are followed.
