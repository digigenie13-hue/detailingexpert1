What is Graphene Coating?

Reduced Graphene Oxide (rGO) Engineering

Graphene Coating is an advanced protective coating that bonds with your vehicle's factory paint to create a durable, transparent layer of protection. It helps guard against UV rays, bird droppings, tree sap, road grime and everyday environmental contaminants while enhancing the depth and gloss of your vehicle's finish.

Compared to traditional ceramic coatings, Graphene Coating offers improved heat dissipation and reduced water spotting, making it an excellent choice for Indian weather conditions. At Detailing Experts, every Graphene Coating application begins with careful paint preparation and is professionally applied at our studios in Vadodara and Godhra for long-lasting performance.
