What is Professional Interior Car Detailing?

Complete Interior Cleaning & Cabin Restoration

Professional Interior Car Detailing is a complete deep-cleaning and restoration process that refreshes every part of your vehicle's cabin. It includes vacuuming, upholstery cleaning, leather conditioning, dashboard cleaning, steam sanitization, odour removal, and interior glass cleaning to restore a clean, hygienic, and comfortable interior.

At Detailing Experts, our Interior Car Detailing service is performed by trained professionals using premium products and professional equipment at our Vadodara and Godhra studios. Whether your vehicle requires routine maintenance or a complete interior restoration, we deliver exceptional cleanliness, comfort, and long-lasting protection.

What's Included in Our Interior Car Detailing Service?

Every Interior Car Detailing service includes a comprehensive cleaning and restoration process designed to refresh, sanitize, and protect every part of your vehicle's cabin.

Complete Interior Vacuuming

Deep vacuuming removes dust, sand, dirt, food particles, and debris from carpets, seats, floor mats, the boot area, and hard-to-reach spaces.

Dashboard Cleaning

We carefully clean the dashboard, instrument cluster, centre console, and interior trims using safe, non-greasy products that leave a clean factory finish.

Door Panel Cleaning

Door panels, armrests, switches, and storage compartments are thoroughly cleaned to remove dust, fingerprints, stains, and everyday grime.

Leather Seat Conditioning

Premium leather cleaners and conditioners restore softness, remove dirt, and help protect leather seats from drying, fading, and cracking.

Fabric Seat Shampoo

Professional upholstery shampoo removes stains, spills, dust, and trapped dirt while helping restore the original appearance of fabric seats.

Roof Lining Cleaning

Gentle cleaning techniques safely remove dust, marks, and light stains from the roof lining without damaging the fabric or adhesive.

Carpet Shampoo

Deep carpet shampooing removes mud, stains, odours, and embedded dirt, leaving carpets fresh, clean, and hygienic.

Floor Mat Cleaning

Rubber and fabric floor mats are thoroughly cleaned, shampooed, and dried to restore a fresh appearance and eliminate trapped dirt.

Plastic Trim Restoration

Interior plastic trims are cleaned and dressed to restore their natural finish while helping protect them from fading and UV exposure.

AC Vent Cleaning

Dust and contaminants are removed from AC vents using specialised tools and steam cleaning to improve cabin hygiene and air quality.

Interior Glass Cleaning

All interior glass surfaces are cleaned to remove fingerprints, haze, and streaks, providing crystal-clear visibility.

Odour Removal

Professional odour removal treatments help eliminate smoke, food, pet, and musty smells, leaving your cabin fresh and pleasant.
