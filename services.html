Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our detailing and protection services.

Ceramic coating is a liquid nano-polymer that chemically bonds with the factory paint to provide extreme gloss, hydrophobicity, and chemical resistance against bird droppings and sap.

Dragon PPF (Paint Protection Film) is a physical, self-healing polyurethane film that offers heavy-duty protection against rock chips, deep scratches, and physical damage.

Choose Ceramic Coating if your main goal is gloss, ease of cleaning, and chemical resistance. Choose PPF if you want physical protection from rock chips and scratches. For ultimate protection, you can apply Ceramic Coating over PPF.

A standard detail takes 4-8 hours. Graphene or Ceramic coating usually takes 1-2 days due to intense paint correction and IR curing. Full PPF installation takes 3-5 days.

Skin Restoration Service (SRS) or paint correction is the process of using dual-action polishers and compounds to physically remove a microscopic layer of clear coat, eliminating swirl marks and scratches.

No coating is entirely scratch-proof. While 9H/10H ceramic coatings are highly scratch-resistant and prevent light swirl marks from washing, they will not protect against keying or rock chips like PPF does.

Yes, all our premium Ceramic and PPF packages come with an official, written warranty ranging from 3 to 7 years covering peeling, yellowing, and loss of gloss.

Graphene coating is an evolution of ceramic coating infused with reduced Graphene Oxide. It offers superior heat dissipation, reducing the chance of water spots significantly.

Yes, we offer specialized superbike and cruiser detailing, including engine degreasing, chrome polishing, and tank ceramic coating.

Absolutely. Dealerships often wash cars improperly, leaving swirl marks. A new car requires a light correction and immediate protection to lock in the factory clear coat.

Wash your car every two weeks using a pH-neutral shampoo and the two-bucket method. Avoid automated brush washes or harsh detergents at local washing centers.

It includes vacuuming, deep steam cleaning, leather cleaning and conditioning, fabric extraction, roof lining cleaning, and AC vent sanitization.

Yes, our headlight restoration service involves wet sanding the oxidized UV layer, compounding it back to clarity, and sealing it with a UV protective ceramic coating.

Over time, pores in the ceramic coating can get clogged with minerals. A maintenance wash uses special chemical decontaminants to unclog the coating and a silica sealant to top up the hydrophobicity.
