Our Detailing Workflow

A clear overview of the typical steps used to assess, clean, correct, protect and prepare a vehicle for delivery.

01

Vehicle Inspection

Reviewing the vehicle and visible surface condition before work begins.

02

Cleaning

Cleaning the vehicle and preparing relevant surfaces for the selected service.

03

Paint Correction

Where required, paint correction is carried out according to the vehicle’s condition and agreed service.

04

Protection

Applying the selected protection service according to the agreed scope of work.

05

Final Inspection

Checking completed work and reviewing the vehicle before handover.

06

Vehicle Delivery

Preparing the vehicle for collection and sharing relevant care guidance where applicable.
