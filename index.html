The Detailing Process

Perfection cannot be rushed. Discover the meticulous steps we take to transform your vehicle.

1

Comprehensive Inspection

We begin with a thorough assessment of your vehicle's paint thickness using digital gauges, identifying deep scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation levels.

2

Decontamination Wash

A pH-neutral snow foam wash followed by clay bar treatment to safely extract embedded iron particles, tar, and industrial fallout from the clear coat.

3

Paint Correction (SRS)

Multi-stage machine compounding and polishing to surgically remove defects. We literally restore the skin of your vehicle to a factory-fresh mirror finish.

4

Coating & IR Curing

Application of 9H/10H Ceramic or Graphene coating in a temperature-controlled dust-free room, followed by Infrared (IR) lamp baking for maximum hardness.

5

Final Delivery

A rigorous multi-point quality check under high-intensity inspection lights. Your vehicle is delivered with a pristine finish and official warranty documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium detailing and protection services.

Ceramic coating is a liquid nano-polymer that chemically bonds with the factory paint to provide extreme gloss, hydrophobicity, and chemical resistance against bird droppings and sap. PPF (Paint Protection Film) is a physical, self-healing polyurethane film that offers heavy-duty protection against rock chips, deep scratches, and physical damage. Many clients opt to apply ceramic coating OVER PPF for the ultimate combination.

A standard premium exterior and interior detail (Skin Restoration) takes about 4-8 hours. Graphene or Ceramic coating usually takes 1-2 days due to the intensive paint correction and IR curing time required. Full body Grace Shield PPF installation can take 3-5 days to ensure perfect edge-wrapping and curing.

Maintenance is significantly easier! We recommend washing the car every 2 weeks using a pH-neutral shampoo and the two-bucket method. Avoid automated brush car washes as they cause micro-scratches. We also offer annual maintenance packages to top-up the coating's hydrophobic properties.

Yes, absolutely. All our Ceramic Coating and PPF packages come with an official, written warranty ranging from 3 to 7 years depending on the tier selected. This covers yellowing, cracking, peeling, and loss of gloss under normal maintenance conditions.
