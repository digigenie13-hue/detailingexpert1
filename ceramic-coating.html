Maintenance Guidelines

  • The Two-Bucket Clean Method: Use separate wash and rinse bucket pairs to isolate sand debris particles.
  • pH Neutral Shampoo Blends: Clean using paint soaps free from gloss conditioners or aggressive silicon additives.
  • Avoid Automatic Car Washes: Avoid stiff mechanical rotating spin brushes that run the risk of introducing micro-marring flaws.

Industry Myths vs Facts

Myth: Ceramic coatings protect panels from high-speed rock chips.

Fact: They resist fine micro-swirls. Preventing fast stone impacts requires an application of Dragon PPF.

Myth: Coated vehicles never require manual washing ever again.

Fact: The topcoat becomes highly self-cleaning, but occasional gentle hand wash sessions remain vital.
