Car Spa Process

Our systematic approach ensures perfection at every stage of the wash and spa.

1

Inspection

We start with a walk-around inspection to identify heavily soiled areas, stains, and custom requirements for your vehicle.

2

Foam Wash

A luxurious blanket of pH-neutral snow foam is applied to encapsulate dirt, followed by a gentle hand wash.

3

Deep Cleaning

Focus shifts to the interior and engine bay. Using steam and specialized brushes to agitate and extract grime.

4

Drying

Safe, scratch-free drying using ultra-plush microfiber towels and forced warm air to blow water out of crevices.

5

Polishing

Application of premium waxes, sealants, and dressings to the paint, dashboard, and tyres to enhance shine and protect.

6

Final Quality Check

A comprehensive multi-point inspection under high-intensity lights ensures every inch meets our premium standards.
