Car Spa Process

Our six-step process keeps the wash and spa service clear, careful and consistent.

1

Inspection

We start with a walk-around inspection to identify heavily soiled areas, stains, and custom requirements for your vehicle.

2

Foam Wash

Foam is applied to help loosen surface dirt before careful hand cleaning and rinsing.

3

Deep Cleaning

Selected interior areas are vacuumed and cleaned according to the chosen package, with deeper specialist treatments handled separately.

4

Drying

The vehicle is carefully dried, including attention to accessible edges and water-trap areas.

5

Finishing Touches

Package-specific finishing touches, such as wax or tyre dressing, are completed where included. Paint defect removal is available through Paint Correction.

6

Final Quality Check

The completed service is reviewed before handover, with attention to the areas covered by the selected package.
