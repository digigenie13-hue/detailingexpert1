What is Professional Engine Bay Detailing?

Complete Engine Bay Cleaning & Protection

Professional Engine Bay Detailing is a specialised cleaning service that removes grease, oil, dust, dirt, and road grime from your vehicle's engine compartment. It helps restore a clean, well-maintained engine bay while improving overall appearance and making routine inspections easier.

At Detailing Experts, we use safe cleaning techniques, premium degreasers, controlled steam cleaning, and specialised detailing tools to clean engine components without using excessive water pressure. Our Engine Bay Detailing service is available at our Vadodara and Godhra studios for all types of vehicles, from hatchbacks and sedans to SUVs and luxury cars.

Sensitive Component Protection

Professional Products & Equipment We Use

Premium Engine Degreaser

Engine Dressing

Microfiber Towels

Premium microfiber towels safely remove residue, excess dressing, and remaining moisture without scratching or leaving lint behind.

Plastic & Rubber Protectant

Premium protectants restore plastic covers and rubber components to a clean, natural finish while helping reduce fading and drying.

Brush Set

Professional detailing brushes allow safe cleaning of intricate engine components, tight gaps, hoses, and hard-to-reach areas.

Air Blower

Professional air blowers remove residual moisture from hard-to-reach areas, helping ensure the engine bay is clean and properly dried before final finishing.

Strict Safety Mitigation Standards

We protect your electrical architecture with careful clean-room rules.

Safe Electrical Protection

All core electrical assemblies, alternator modules, and exposed battery terminals are securely wrapped inside moisture-barrier membranes before processing lines execute.

Low Pressure Cleaning

Sensitive Component Care

Eco Friendly Chemicals
