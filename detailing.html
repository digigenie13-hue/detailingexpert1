About Detailing

Car detailing is the process of thoroughly cleaning and restoring a vehicle to its original condition. It is an art of enhancing the overall look and feel of your vehicle by removing swirl marks, scratches, and dullness from the exterior paint, while deeply sanitizing the interior.

  • Enhances the resale value of the vehicle.
  • Restores original factory shine and gloss.
  • Protects exterior clear coat from environmental damage.
  • Ensures a hygienic and odor-free interior cabin.

Our Detailing Process

1. Foam Wash & Decontamination

Deep cleaning to safely remove surface dirt, iron particles, and tar without scratching the paint.

2. Paint Correction (Skin Restoration)

Multi-stage machine compounding to eliminate swirl marks, oxidation, and deep scratches.

3. Interior Deep Cleaning

Steam cleaning and sanitization of fabric, leather, dashboard, and AC vents.

4. Final Polishing & Finishing

Restoring chrome trims, headlights, and alloys to bring back the showroom shine.

Skin Restoration Service

In Skin Restoration Service (SRS), the surface of the vehicle is carefully buffed to get rid of the old and dull layer of the paint to reveal a brand new, vibrant, and shiny layer underneath. This is crucial before applying any ceramic coating or PPF.

Our Detailed Restoration Includes:

  • Paint Correction: Removing deep scratches and swirl marks.
  • Headlight Restoration: Bringing back clarity.
  • Chrome Restoration: Polishing to a mirror finish.
  • Wheel Restoration: Deep cleaning alloys.
